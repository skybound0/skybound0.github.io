My friend and I struggle to beat another friend at the word games on GamePigeon, so we're building a CoreXY stylus-manipulator to do it for us.

It uses OpenCV to read the phone screen, Python scripts to do the math, and Klipper to control the motion.

The end goal is to expand it to other games as well, like 8-Ball or even general mobile games.
